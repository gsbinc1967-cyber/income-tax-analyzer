# Income Tax Act Section Mappings (ITA 1961 to ITA 2025)

## Key Concepts
- **ITA 1961**: Applicable up to FY 2025-26 (AY 2026-27).
- **ITA 2025**: Applicable from FY 2026-27 (AY 2027-28) onwards.

## Important Section Mappings
- **Charging Section**: Sec 4 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 3 (ITA 2025)
- **Residential Status**: Sec 6 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 5 (ITA 2025)
- **Income from Salaries**: Sec 15-17 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 14-16 (ITA 2025)
- **Income from House Property**: Sec 22-27 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 20-25 (ITA 2025)
- **Profits and Gains of Business/Profession (PGBP)**: Sec 28-44 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 28-60 (ITA 2025)
- **Capital Gains**: Sec 45-55 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 61-70 (ITA 2025)
- **Income from Other Sources**: Sec 56-59 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 71-74 (ITA 2025)
- **Clubbing of Income**: Sec 60-64 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 75-79 (ITA 2025)
- **Set off and Carry Forward of Losses**: Sec 70-80 (ITA 1961) -> Sec 80-90 (ITA 2025)
- **Chapter VI-A Deductions**: Sec 80C to 80U (ITA 1961) -> Chapter VI‑A (ITA 2025, largely retained but with updated clause numbers)
- **Rebate**: Sec 87A (ITA 1961) -> Sec 87A (ITA 2025)
- **Assessment/Notices**: 
  - Intimation: Sec 143(1) (1961) -> Sec 143(1) (2025)
  - Scrutiny: Sec 143(2) (1961) -> Sec 143(2) (2025)
  - Reassessment: Sec 147/148 (1961) -> Sec 147/148 (2025)

## TDS Consolidation (Key Change)
- **Salary TDS**: Previously Sec 192 → **Sec 392** (ITA 2025)
- **All Non‑Salary TDS** (interest, rent, contractor, professional fees, royalty, etc.): Previously scattered across Sec 193‑194‑T, 195, 196, 206C → **Sec 393** (ITA 2025) with a **Nature‑Code table** (e.g., 1001 = Interest on deposits, 1005 = Rent – Residential, 1012 = Professional fees, 1023 = Contractor – Civil work, 1055 = Crypto cash settlement, etc.).
- **Payments to NRIs/foreign entities**: Previously Sec 195 → now covered under Sec 393 (Nature‑Code = 1060) with DTAA‑adjusted rates.
- **Missing PAN/Aadhaar higher rates**: Previously Sec 206C(1B)/(2) → now enforced via Sec 393 Nature‑Code = 1065.
- **New provisions** (crypto, e‑wallet high‑value payments) are added as sub‑codes under Sec 393 (e.g., 1055, 1056).
